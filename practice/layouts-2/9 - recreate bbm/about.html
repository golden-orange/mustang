Professional Info

I am a certified, professional makeup artist and the principal of Beauty by Masayo. My base is the Greater Toronto Area though am available to travel to project locations.

I am certified, with a diploma in Creative & Corrective Makeup Design from the School of Professional Makeup in Toronto, Canada. I also pursue ongoing training in traditional Japanese makeup techniques in Tokyo, Japan.

As an artistic coordinator, my success depends heavily upon solving problems for clients and utilizing strong organization, communication and ‘soft’ people skills.

Corporate and cultural events

Uniquely skilled in authentic traditional Japanese makeup and styling. Specifically – maiko, geisha and kabuki.

My team includes a professional kimono dresser, trained in Japan in authentic kimono application.

Together, we bring unique and authentic cultural experience to your organization’s cultural events.
