My name is Masayo Laing and I am a Professional and Certified Makeup Artist. My passion is working with other professionals creating interesting and quality makeup solutions in a wide variety of project settings.

My team is comprised of certified and experienced professionals. These include a professionally trained hairstylist and a professionally trained kimono dresser.

As a paid professional, my ongoing projects involve fashion catalogs, professional speakers and models, photo galleries, workshops and more.

I also work on music videos, independent film sets, theatre productions and cultural events.

My work and involvement in the industry spans international borders and, so far, includes North America and Asia.

I provide makeup and styling solutions that meet a wide range of needs. However, I do have a special niche in authentic, traditional Japanese makeup and kimono styling.

For this I trained in Japan with professional studios dedicated to teaching this tradition. Whether I am creating a Geisha or Kabuki theme, the results are always the real deal. I take my education seriously and so continue to return to Japan for ongoing instruction.

I am principally a makeup artist. However, some projects require me to be an artistic coordinator and event organizer. Whether or not you need someone to fill those roles, I believe these qualifications give me an edge in creating a professional work experience on all my projects.

I am always seeking new opportunities to work with other fashion professionals.
