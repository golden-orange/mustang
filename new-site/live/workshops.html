Work With Professionals

My workshops allow you to work on your craft in a professional and creative environment. The photographers are available to offer pointers on camera use and suggestions to help you make the most of your window of opportunity with the model.

Ample Time Guaranteed

  • ★ 7 openings per workshop
  • ★ 2 photo sessions with model per participant
  • ★ 3 to 5 minutes per photo session

I limit the number of participants to around six to seven per workshop session. This will enable you at least a few individual shoots with the model in approximately three minute durations.

Friendly and Helpful Professionals

  • ★ All levels of experience welcomed and encouraged
  • ★ work with professional and courteous photographers and models

If you are somewhat new to photoshoots, help is available from our photographer in lens and camera work, how to work with lighting, communicate effectively and efficiently with models and more.

My photographers are professional and friendly. No one will try to impose upon you any particular method. Expect to be encouraged to explore your own ideas and techniques.

Gain experience directing models

I work with models directly and look for those offering the complete package: photogeneity and variety, intelligence and focus and reliability. As a photographer, you will find my models will contribute enormously to the success of your photoshoot experience.

Variety of Locations

I like to keep the settings for workshops fresh and interesting, and use alternate venues, including:

  • ★ Professional Studios
  • ★ Outdoor Locations
  • ★ Private Residences

Our studios always have modern, state-of-the-art facilities, including professional lighting and variety in backdrops.

During summer months we take advantage of the good weather as much as possible. Toronto has many street and park locations that add extra flavour to your photo sessions.

Unique settings such as private residences provide photoshoots with a truly authentic feel.
