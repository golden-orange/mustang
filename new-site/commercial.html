Professional Headshots

for professionals. by professionals.

We are a team of experienced, fashion industry professionals. We have many years’ experience invested in capturing the looks people want – film, music video, live venues.

And, yes, we also provide headshots for professionals.

We are more expensive that what else is out there. But we aren’t competing with discount photographers. The differences in results are well worth it. And we couldn’t do it any other way.

What makes a picture speak a thousands words?

The live quality. Freshness. The camera lens sees and records all, without judgement. Makeup and hairstyling, combined with effective lighting techniques, posing and angles are all essential to creating the look that is both real, and the one you want. It all doesn’t “just happen”.

No professional goes in front of a camera without details like makeup and hair well under control. Neither should you.
